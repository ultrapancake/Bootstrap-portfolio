About Me

My name is Glen Larsen, I am currently the meat department manager at Smith's Food and Drug Store in the Avenues, Salt Lake City Utah. I have been a butcher for 10 years now, 2 of which i have been managing my own shop. I absolutely love my job; however, I have reached the peak of my long term goals. I am looking forward to starting a new career where I am able to set, and achieve, both short and long term goals.
In my person life I have two beautiful young boys ages 6 and 7. My wife and I recently purchased a new home and are slowly improving it every year, this year was a new roof.
